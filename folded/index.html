FOLDED: Origami Conference
February 8, 2014
Palmer Events Center
Austin, Texas

Any way you fold it...

"Paper folding is beautiful, and immortal, thanks to its simplicity. After all, there is only one way to fold a piece of paper. Yet within that single fold is a universe of possibilities. With every variation of a fold, a new shape emerges. In a common sheet of paper, there exists the notion of a flower, the thought of a fish, and the dream of a bird." —Creased Magazine

Join us this year at FOLDED as we bring to you some of the most brilliant and innovative origami artists to share their tips and techniques. Get your fold on during our speed folding contests, or step back and observe Mademoiselle Maurice do a live installation of her origami street art.

Guest Speakers

Eric Josiel

Eric Joisel was a French origami artist who specialized in the wet-folding method creating figurative art sculptures.

Joel Cooper

Paper artist Joel Cooper folds these astounding masks and tessellations from single pieces of paper.

Robert J. Lang

Robert J. Lang is an American physicist who is also one of the foremost origami artists and theorists in the world.
